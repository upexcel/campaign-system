About Excellence RMS

Add jobs, review applications, schedule interview and hire, all at one place - Excellence RMS. More then Application tracking system. A Recruitment Management System (RMS) is a set of tools designed to automate and manage your organization's recruiting and staffing operations. From adding your jobs to keeping application connected and engaged throughout, Excellence RMS lets you manage your entire recruiting process.

Features

  • Extensive integration: Recruit Straight from portal or inbox
  • Automated Workflow: Post jobs, View Resumes, Send Notification & more
  • Application Tracking: Search, Screen & Select Candidate easily
  • Database Management: organize all candidates data & files securely
  • Smart Automation: Define automation rules for every job
  • Interview Scheduler
  • Integrated E-mail conversations
